Thesis

Frictional Behavious of HDPE, PVC, PMMA Polymers

In this thesis, we’ve selected HDPE, PVC, PMMA(two types) as polymeric material and are measuring the frictional properties of them at different sliding velocity. Frictional behavior of these polymeric materials mainly depend on the sliding velocity and temperature.

We’ve modified a tribometer which works in the principle of disc and pin method to measure the frictional coefficient of these materials. PMMA is used as the pin in all observations while the discs were varied. We measure the fiction coefficient and temperature of the contact point of pin and disc for different polymer combinations.
